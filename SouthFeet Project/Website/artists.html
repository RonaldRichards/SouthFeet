Our Artists:

Meet the voices defining SouthFeet’s sound.

Mike T

Born Michael Thabo Timile, professionally known as Mike.T, is an emerging artist from Johannesburg, South Africa. His journey into music began professionally during his final years of high school. Having always felt a natural pull toward the artform, he believes music is something he ultimately found his way back to. At 22, he released his debut EP, View: Extended Visions, in December 2022 a project largely self produced. Showcasing his evolving creativity and distinct perspective, Mike.T now returns with his latest project, 24 Hours, available to stream and download.

Joey Yams

Joey Yams is a South African rapper and record-producer. Born Tshiamo Mokatsane and raised in the southern region of Johannesburg, he discovered his passion for music early on. Joey began his musical journey at just 14 years old, drawing major influence from artists such as A-Reece, Nasty C, Kendrick Lamar and Joey-Bada$$. His lyricism is recognized for its technical precision, impressive breath control, and his use of intricate internal and multi-syllabic rhyme schemes. His music spans several hip-hop subgenres, including horrorcore, comedy hip-hop, and hardcore hip-hop. Joey also incorporates alter-egos into his work to explore different styles and themes. His most notable alter-ego, “Sketchy-Scully,” delivers-dark, violent, and twistedly comedic narratives, showcasing another layer of his creativity and versatility as an artist.

Rich Official

Rich Official is a South African beat-maker and record producer. Born Ronald Kabelo Richards, he was raised in a township in the south of Johannesburg, where his passion for music first took shape. Rich began his musical journey at the age of 16 as a part-time DJ,a path that eventually led him to discover his love for music production. His sound has been shaped by notable influences such as MashBeatz, DJ-Dre, Herc-Cut-The-Lights, and Boi-1da. His production style can best be described as soulful and emotive, while still being able to seamlessly tap into the modern trap bounce when required. Rich Official recently produced Mike.T’s latest project, 24-Hours, and contributed to Joey Yams’ debut EP, YAMS.

MKshft

Mkshft, born Meso Kgosinkwe, is a producer and songwriter from Kagiso, on the west side of Johannesburg. Known for his distinctive sonic identity, he has developed a sound that is soft, atmospheric, and emotionally rich, yet capable of shifting into a raw, hard hitting trap and hip-hop edge when needed. Driven by experimentation and a deep understanding of rhythm and texture, Meso blends contrasting elements to create music that is both intimate and powerful. His versatility allows him to move seamlessly between smooth, melodic production and gritty, high impact beats making his work stand out within South Africa’s evolving music landscape. With a growing contritubtuion to the SouthFeet catalogue and a reputation for innovation, Meso Kgosinkwe (Mkshft) continues to refine his sound while collaborating with artists who value originality, depth, and boundary pushing creativity.
